Pelmeni

Back to homepage

Description

Pelmeni are small Russian-style meat dumplings, traditionally served with sour cream.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 egg
  • 250ml water
  • 2tsp salt
  • 300g flour (or as much as it takes to make dough)

Filling:

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250g minced meat
  • salt
  • pepper

Garnish

  • Sour cream (or mayonnaise if your name is Boris)
  • Dill or green onion

Steps

Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, add one egg and 250ml water. Whisk the ingredients.
  2. Add salt. Continue whisking.
  3. Add some flour and continue to whisk. Keep adding flour until the ingredients become dough.
  4. Remove the dough. On a floured surface, knead the dough for a minute.
  5. Cover the dough with a bowl or plastic wrap and let it sit for 20-30 minutes.

Filling:

  1. Finely chop or blend the onion and garlic. Make the pieces as small as possible!
  2. Add minced meat, onion and garlic to a bowl and knead together with a spoon or your hands.

Making pelmeni:

  1. Take your dough and roll it out as thinly as possible. It's best to flour your rolling pin and surface.
  2. For pelmeni skins, cut out palm-sized circles in the dough using a cup or something similar.
  3. Take a circle and put enough meat so that there is enough space around the edge of the circle.
  4. Fold the pelmeni skin in half and pinch the edge together. (Or close it however you like.)
  5. Repeat from step one to finish making all pelmeni.
  6. Fill a well-sized pot halfway with water and add salt. Set it to boil.
  7. Once the water is at a boil, insert the pelmeni into the water with a slotted spoon/ladle.
  8. Boil for 7 minutes, shuffling the pelmeni around occasionally.
  9. After boiling, scoop out the pelmeni with the slotted spoon/ladle and put them on a plate.
  10. Dress the pelmeni with a generous amount of sour cream or mayonnaise on top, and garnish with a sprinkle of dill or green onion.
