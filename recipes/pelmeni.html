Pelmeni

Description

Pelmeni are small Russian-style meat dumplings, traditionally served with mayonnaise.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 egg
  • 250ml water
  • 2tsp salt
  • 300g flour (or as much as it takes to make dough)

Filling:

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250g minced meat
  • salt
  • pepper

Garnish

  • Sour cream (or mayonnaise if your name is Boris)
  • Dill or green onion
