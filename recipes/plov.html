Plov

Description

Ingredients

  • 300g meat (whatever you like)
  • 300g rice
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 whole garlic
  • 3 carrots
  • Sunflower seed oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Parsley (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into chunks of your preferred size.
  2. Add a thin layer of oil to a medium size pot.
  3. Put the pot on the oven and set it to boil.
  4. When the oil is hot, add the meat chunks to oil.
  5. While the pot is boiling, cut up the onion into big pieces. Add the onion to the pot and mix the contents around gently.
  6. Cut up the carrots into quarter logs (so they survive cooking). Add them to the pot and mix.
  7. Peel the bulb of garlic. You don't need to break it apart.
  8. Wash the rice, if you'd like.
  9. Add your preferred amount of salt to the pot.
  10. Add water to the pot until the ingredients start floating.
  11. Put the bulb of garlic in the middle of the pot.
  12. Let it boil for around 10 minutes, until the water level returns to what it was before.
  13. Remove the garlic bulb from the pot and set it aside.
  14. Carefully add rice to the top to create a surface of rice. No more mixing after this point!
  15. Set the stove to low heat. Spread out the rice evenly in the pot. (Try not to mix the layers of ingredients together.)
  16. Add water until there is about 2cm of water above rice. (Try not to mix the layers of ingredients together.)
  17. Let the pot sit until the water evaporates. (You can mix the rice while waiting, but don't go too deep.)
  18. When water is gone, push the garlic bulb deep into the middle of the rice. Cover it with a bit of rice.
  19. Poke 3 holes in the rice with a stick.
  20. Cover pot partially with small gap. Cover the pot with a towel before using the lid, if you'd like.
  21. Let sit for 5 min.
  22. Open the pot and stir the rice around. (Remember not to mix the top and bottom layers.) Add water if needed.
  23. Poke holes in the rice and cover the pot again. This time let it sit for 20 min.
  24. You're done! Finally, you can mix everything together. Serve in a bowl with a leaf of parsley on top.
